Webinar: The Case for Doing Something Different

– How and why you should create original customer experiences

Who: Søren Langkjer Ravn
What: Webinar: The Case for Doing Something Different – How and Why you Should Create Original Customer Experiences
When: 11th December, 11:00 am – 12:00 pm
Where: Online

Everybody wants to create great customer experiences. But there is actually real value in thinking about the task in a different way. Of course experiences should be great, but they should also make you stand out from your competitors – they should be differentiated. In this webinar, Søren Ravn from Responsive, will take you through how to think differently about CX, by applying key ingredients such as your brand and your data to create experiences your customers will remember.

Af: Søren Langkjer Ravn

Head of creative strategy, Responsive
